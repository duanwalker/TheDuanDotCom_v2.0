Home of "The Computer Tutor"

Hi I'm Duan! But you can call me "The Computer Tutor". I am a seasoned, full stack web developer, problem solver, full time Panthers fan and all around awesome guy in Charlotte, NC. I have contributed to the full SDLC (software development lifecycle) from requirements gathering to deployment on multiple corporate, freelance and open source projects. Currently, I work as a Cloud Support Engineer for Microsoft. In my free time, I design innovative, mobile friendly web solutions using tools such as C#.Net, ASP.Net, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and BootStrap . I also have a deep passion for technology and teaching others about it. Check out some of the cool stuff I've done, or view my skills and feel free to reach out via LinkedIn or just drop me a message if you have a project you would like some help with!
